Who We Are

EnAbled Foundation is a grassroots organisation that empowers people with disabilities by creating opportunities, telling stories, and amplifying voices to promote independence and inclusion. We believe disability is not inability but rather being abled differently.

For too long, disabled people have been ignored and excluded by society without looking at their potential. This must change. Through advocacy, skills development, and community support, we ensure that people with disabilities thrive with dignity, respect, and equal access to life’s opportunities.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

To rewrite the narrative of disability from limitation to leadership, making inclusion the rule not the exception.

Our Vision

A connected world where every ability has a seat at the table and a voice in the conversation.
